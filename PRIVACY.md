# Privacy Policy (Summary)

This extension generates summaries for YouTube videos by sending relevant page content (title, description, and transcript when available) to the Gemini API.

## Data processed

- From the YouTube page: video title, description text, and (if available) transcript.
- Video URL.

## How the data is used

- The data is used only to generate the summary.
- The extension is not designed to send your data to any custom server operated by the extension author.

## Local storage

- Your API key and a few UI preferences are stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`.

## Third parties

- Summarization is performed via the Google Gemini API (`generativelanguage.googleapis.com`).
- Gemini-side processing/retention policies are subject to Google’s terms.

## Contact

- It is recommended to provide a support/contact link on your Opera store listing (support page).
